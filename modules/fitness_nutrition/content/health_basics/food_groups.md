# Food Groups

Our bodies need different kinds of food to stay healthy. Here are the main groups:

## Fruits and Vegetables

- Give us vitamins and minerals
- Help us stay strong and healthy
- Try to eat many colors each day

## Grains

- Bread, rice, oats
- Give us energy
- Whole grains are best

## Protein

- Meat, fish, eggs, beans, nuts
- Help our muscles grow
- Repair our body

## Dairy (or alternatives)

- Milk, yogurt
- Good for bones
- If you can't have dairy, there are other options

## Balance

- Eat a little from each group
- No single food has everything
- Variety is key
