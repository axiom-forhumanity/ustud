# Introduction to Addition

Addition means putting things together. When we add, we combine two or more numbers to get a bigger number.

## The Plus Sign (+)

The symbol **+** means "add" or "plus". For example: 2 + 3 = 5

## Simple Examples

- 1 + 1 = 2 (one apple plus one apple is two apples)
- 2 + 2 = 4
- 3 + 2 = 5
- 4 + 1 = 5
- 5 + 5 = 10

## Key Idea

When you add, you are counting all the things together. Start from the first number and count up by the second number.

Example: 3 + 4 = ?
- Start at 3
- Count up 4 more: 4, 5, 6, 7
- Answer: 7
