# Living and Non-Living Things

Everything around us is either living or non-living.

## Living Things

Living things:
- Grow and change
- Need food and water
- Can have babies or make new plants
- Move (even plants move slowly toward light)

Examples: people, animals, plants, insects.

## Non-Living Things

Non-living things:
- Do not grow
- Do not need food
- Do not have babies

Examples: rocks, water, chairs, books, the sun.

## How to Tell

Ask: Does it grow? Does it need food? Can it have babies? If yes, it is living.
