# Greetings and Polite Words

When we meet people, we use special words. These words are important in every language.

## Greetings

- **Hello** - A friendly way to say hi
- **Good morning** - Used before noon
- **Good afternoon** - Used after noon
- **Good evening** - Used at night
- **Goodbye** - When we leave

## Polite Words

- **Please** - When we ask for something
- **Thank you** - When someone helps us
- **You're welcome** - Reply to thank you
- **Sorry** - When we make a mistake
- **Excuse me** - To get attention politely

## Example

"Hello! How are you?"
"Please pass the book."
"Thank you for your help."
