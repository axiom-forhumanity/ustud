# What Are Feelings?

Feelings are how we experience the world inside us. Everyone has feelings. They are normal and important.

## Common Feelings

- **Happy** - When something good happens
- **Sad** - When we lose something or feel down
- **Angry** - When something feels unfair
- **Scared** - When we sense danger
- **Calm** - When we feel safe and peaceful
- **Excited** - When we look forward to something

## It's OK to Feel

All feelings are valid. There is no "wrong" feeling. What matters is what we do with them.

## Naming Feelings

When we can name our feelings, we understand ourselves better. Try saying: "I feel ___ right now."
