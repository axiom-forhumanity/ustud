## NVIDIA DLI — how to enroll

1. Go to https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/training/self-paced-courses/
2. Use **Free Courses** filter or search course title
3. Click **View Course** → **Enroll Now**
4. Sign in with your NVIDIA Developer account
5. Complete at your own pace; many courses include a **certificate** — save a screenshot to your Week 1 upload folder

### Recommended free courses (priority order)

1. **Generative AI Explained** — What generative AI is, how LLMs work, use cases (~2 hr). **Start Wednesday.**

2. **Augment Your LLM Using RAG** — How AI retrieves documents to answer questions. Directly related to UStud's tutor + library. **Thursday optional stretch.**

3. **AI for All: From Basics to GenAI Practice** — Broader intro if Generative AI Explained feels too fast (search on NVIDIA Academy / DLI catalog).

Browse the full free catalog: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/training/self-paced-courses/ → **Show Me All Free Courses**
